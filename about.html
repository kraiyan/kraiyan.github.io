This is me...

...born in Bavaria, I've lived in several different countries (Austria, Chile, USA, UK, Netherlands, and Mexico) before coming to Berlin. My experience is in a variety of industries and roles, where I've had the opportunity to develop my skills in problem solving, working as part of a team and analytical skills. For example in my role as Office Manager at a start-up, I was used to figuring out pain points and how to solve them quickly, often in creative ways. I've worked with a variety of cultures, which allowed me to learn how to communicate effectively and without fear.

I'm transitioning to software development because this is where I know I can make an even bigger impact, being solution focused and enjoying connecting dots and people.

My values:

Being honest and open-minded

I strongly believe that being your authentic self is the best for everyone. This way we can bring all our unique talents and characteristics to the table and create something awesome. We can learn something from EVERYONE.

Owning it

I love to challenge myself and being challenged. Looking beyond the box is essential to find an even better and efficient way of doing things. Everyone makes mistakes, owning them and learning from them is how I grow.

Team spirit

Working as a team is how we can create synergies and momentum to overcome obstacles. However, being part of a team also means both to be able to work independently and carry my own weight and to ask for help when I'm stuck.
